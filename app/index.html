Namesake

Why the name Albatross?

We get this question a lot. The fact of the matter is, the albatross caught a bad rep. These magnficient birds have the largest wingspan of any living species, measuring up to 12 feet wide. By utilizing a technique called dynamic soaring, an albatross can fly over 9,000 miles without touching land. Some species can even desalinate water and maintain flight while asleep.
