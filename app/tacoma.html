Port of Tacoma

The Ease of Doing Business

The Port of Tacoma is America's third-largest port on the West Coast, and growing quickly. We were brought on to overhaul the site's information architecture and create a visual design that better reflected their recent re-branding initiative.

Challenge

The old site had become bloated with content, which made finding information difficult for potential and current clients. The Port needed a modern, responsive website that highlighted their friendly, professional attitude and key competitve advantages.

Interactive Features

Our strategy was to integrate a host of user-focused features such as searchable databases, interactive maps, and carrier feeds into an elegant, responsive Drupal theme. We identified early on that the Ports' advantages were centered around Shipping, Real Estate, and Community. Anchored by large images and a strong font selection, we designed a system of content to better frame the wealth of information about the Port.

Results

With a variety of interactive features and a refreshed, responsive design, bounce rates were lowered and mobile traffic as well as overall length of visit were significantly increased. The Port's constituents and customers are now accessing new information, even in transit.
